Why Rukn

Built on the pillar of language.

Glass Morphism

Frosted glass surfaces, light refraction effects, and translucent depth layers that give your UI a premium, physical feel.

Rukn Motion

iOS-inspired easing curves and spring physics for buttery animations.

150+ Tokens

W3C-compliant design tokens covering color, spacing, typography, shadows, and motion.

RTL First

Arabic and Urdu layouts that just work. dir="rtl" flips everything — no patches needed.

Dark & Light

Toggle themes with a single class. Tokens adapt automatically.

Get started

Two lines.
